The VisitIQ Method

Prepare. Capture. Translate.

A three-step system that turns field activity into enterprise intelligence.

01

Prepare

AI surfaces facility insights, referral history, and dynamic coaching prompts tailored to each account before you walk in the door. Arrive with a strategic brief in hand—custom talking points, relationship context, and program-specific intelligence—so every visit starts with purpose and ends with progress.

02

Capture

Record and coach visits in real-time with intelligent tools that summarize, extract key takeaways, and structure your notes instantly. Prefer to work after the visit? Input your raw field notes and let AI optimize, format, and prepare them for CRM entry—no detail lost, no matter how you work.

03

Translate

Convert field activity into enriched, CRM-ready data and referral performance visibility—automatically, with no manual re-entry. Every visit becomes a structured intelligence asset: account health signals, follow-up actions, and strategic insights delivered directly where your team needs them most.
